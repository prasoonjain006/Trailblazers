Complete list of coronavirus (Covid-19) testing centers in India

News March 21, 2020March 25, 2020 indica
ShareTweet
Subscribe

The Indian Council of Medical Research has given a notification where a complete list of the testing centers in India has been given for Coronavirus testing. This list includes all the testing centers for the Covid-19 virus in all the Indian states and the Union territories.

A total of 62 Virus and Research laboratories have been set up by the Indian government. The government is following the advice from the World Health Organization on Coronavirus testing centers.

The ICMR or the Indian Council of Medical Research based in Delhi is the top most body which is responsible for all the biomedical research in the country.  

The Covid-19 cases are spreading fast in India and the current number of cases is nearing 300. There have been 4 confirmed deaths in the country on account of the virus.

India will organize a Janata Curfew on Sunday, 22nd March to contain the spread of the virus.
